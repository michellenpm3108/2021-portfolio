about me

I'm Michelle, a multidisciplinary designer based in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia.

I approach design empathetically and focus on understanding the latent needs and emotions of the people who I am designing for, aiming to create clear, impactful solutions to enrich their lives.

I have worked on projects in UI/UX, web design, branding and identity, signage and print design.

I enjoy learning about new tools and approaches to continue enhancing my design process.
